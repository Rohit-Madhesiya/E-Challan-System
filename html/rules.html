ABOUT US

Maintain safety on the roads with these safe driving tips

  • Carry Your Documents In The Car At All Times
  • Wear Safety Gear While Driving
  • Stick To Speed Limits
  • Keep A Safe Distance With The Car In Front Of You
  • Do Not Drink And Drive
  • Maintain The Car In Good Condition
  • Practise Defensive Driving
  • Avoid Distractions

What not to do to maintain safety on the roads

  • Do not drive without an Insurance Policy
  • Don’t forget to wear a seatbelt
  • Driving without registration papers
  • Not giving way to emergency vehicles
  • Talking on a mobile phone
  • Do Not forget your Driving License
  • Driving when mentally unfit
  • Do not indulge in Racing or Speeding
  • Avoid Unauthorized use of your car
  • Avoid drinking and driving
